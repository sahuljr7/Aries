About

What is Aries?

ARIES is a group of highly motivated students with a passion for space and a mission to impart Space Education, accessible to all. We aim to foster a passion for astronomy and space sciences while encouraging participants to think critically and creatively. We want the young minds of India to get a gateway to the world of space, which we were not fortunate enough to be provided with.

Our Vision

In a world wherein interest knows no bounds, we envision a future in which each individual has the opportunity to explore the wonders of astronomy. Our vision is to provide Stem Education to the students and suppliment the school curriculum with engaging astronomy content. This will ignite the knowledge and passion for astronomy in the students.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to enhance Astronomical learning by providing resources, support and opportunities to obtain space education. We strive to cultivate a new generation of Space enthusiasts and learners with proper fundamentals of space sciences . Our Aim is to foster a deep understanding about our Universe and a lifelong fascination for stars. This learning would help the kids to identify the genius within themselves.
