Frequently Asked Questions

How is the Olympiad beneficial for a participant?
Each student will be provided with a platform to evaluate themself against a large number of other students and understand where they stand with regard to space education.
Who all are eligible for the Olympiad?
School students from classes 6 to 12 and Indian citizens under the age of 35 are eligible for participating in the Olympiad.
How to participate in the Olympiad?
Register for the Olympiad via the registration link provided in the website or through your school.
I am interested to take part in the olympiad. How can I know about the details of the exam?
You will be provided with all the relevant information with regard to the Olympiad from your schools, for more details you can visit our website.
What is the syllabus of the Olympiad?
The detailed syllabus of the Olympiad is provided in the website. The main divisions of the subjects would be Astrophysics, Astrobiology, Astromathematics and Cosmology.
Are there any books or study material available for reference?
We have a booklet which can be used for reference. The link for the purchase is given
What will be the language of conduct of the Olympiad?
The Olympiad will be conducted in English only.
What will be the exam pattern?
The Detailed examination pattern is given on the website. The examination will be completely online.
How can our school/university be a nodal centre?
You can reach out to us at our Email id - cuacindia@gmail.com
If I am an individual participant from a school which has not enrolled yet, will I still be able to participate?
Yes you will be able to be a participant. You can register individually from the website.
