OUR MISSION

Our Mission is to enhance Astronomical learning by providing resources, support and opportunities to obtain space education. We strive to cultivate a new generation of Space enthusiasts and learners with proper fundamentals of space sciences . Our Aim is to foster a deep understanding about our Universe and a lifelong fascination for stars. This learning would help the kids to identify the genius within themselves.

  • FAQ

    How is the Olympiad beneficial for a participant?
    Each student will be provided with a platform to evaluate themself against a large number of other students and understand where they stand with regard to space education.
    Who all are eligible for the Olympiad?
    School students from classes 6 to 12 and Indian citizens under the age of 35 are eligible for participating in the Olympiad.
